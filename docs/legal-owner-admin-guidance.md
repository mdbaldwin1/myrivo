# Legal Owner/Admin Guidance

## When to Publish a New Version
- Material policy changes, jurisdiction changes, or operational risk updates.
- Include a clear `change_summary` and an `effective_at` timestamp.

## Who Should Be Notified
- `terms`: typically all account holders.
- `privacy`: all account holders impacted by data-processing changes.
- Store-specific legal communication can still be sent through store communication channels.

## Evidence to Preserve
- Version metadata (`version_label`, `effective_at`, `published_at`, `published_by_user_id`)
- Notification send evidence (`audit_events`, `notifications`, `notification_delivery_attempts`)
- Acceptance evidence (`legal_acceptances` export)

## Recommended Response SLA
- Initial acknowledgement: within 1 business day.
- Evidence package ready: within 3 business days.
- Dispute resolution handoff: within 5 business days.
