# Cookie Compliance Layer Plan

## Objective

Add a platform-managed cookie compliance layer for public Myrivo surfaces and storefronts.

## First rollout

- cookie inventory and category definitions
- cookie policy page
- shopper-facing banner and preferences center
- consent-gated storefront analytics

## Deliberate non-goals

- store-specific cookie banner customization
- region-based banner suppression
- third-party tag/pixel management
- broader dashboard analytics consent handling

## Ownership

Platform-owned:

- cookie categories
- cookie policy copy
- consent persistence
- analytics gating

Storefronts inherit the shared platform behavior. Stores do not define their own cookie categories in this rollout.
