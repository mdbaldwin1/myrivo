## Domain Setup
Use Store Settings > Domains to add and verify your custom storefront domain. Treat domain setup as part of launch readiness, not as a cosmetic final step.

Before switching traffic:

- verify the storefront on the platform-hosted URL first
- verify your legal pages and policy links resolve correctly
- confirm your email and support contact paths are current

## Launch Checklist
Do not consider the store launch-ready until the basic browse-to-buy path works on mobile and desktop.

- homepage, products, cart, and checkout all render correctly
- custom branding and footer/legal links are present
- payment, pickup, or shipping flows are tested
- policies and legal documents are published
