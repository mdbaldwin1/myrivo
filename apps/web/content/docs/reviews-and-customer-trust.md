## Review Workflow
Use the Reviews page to triage pending reviews, respond where appropriate, and keep the storefront review surface healthy.

What to watch regularly:

- pending moderation queue
- verified-purchase indicators
- image/media attachments
- owner responses on high-signal reviews

## Trust Guidelines
Reviews are part of the storefront trust layer. The goal is not to remove negative sentiment; it is to remove abusive or invalid content while keeping legitimate customer feedback visible.

Use moderation to:

- remove policy-violating content
- correct clearly invalid submissions
- respond to customer concerns with context
