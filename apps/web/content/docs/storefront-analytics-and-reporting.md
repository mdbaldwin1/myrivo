## Where To Read Performance
Use the store workspace analytics page at `/dashboard/stores/:storeSlug/analytics` for storefront traffic and conversion performance.

Use Reports for supporting operational context:

- Insights for broader store performance
- Inventory Ledger for stock movement diagnostics
- Billing Events for fee and settlement visibility

## Questions This Surface Should Answer
Treat analytics as decision support, not decoration. Review the page to answer:

- which pages or product surfaces attract traffic
- where shoppers drop out of the funnel
- which CTAs are pulling people into cart and checkout
- which products or sections get attention without conversion
